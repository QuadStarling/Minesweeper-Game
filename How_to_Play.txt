To play Minesweeper

   1. On the Game menu, click New.
   2. To start the timer. click any square on the playing field
   3. To choose a difficulty mode, on the Game menu,
      click any of the three difficulties.

Notes

   - You can uncover a square by clicking it.
     If you uncover a mine, you lose the game.

   - If a number appears on a square,
     it indicates how many mines are in the eight squares
     that surround the numbered one.

   - To mark a square you suspect contains a mine,
     right-click it.

   - The game area consists of the playing field,
     a mine counter, and a timer.
